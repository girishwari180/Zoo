Meet Our Animals

Learn about our animals, their safety rules, and feeding habits

Gorilla

  • Highly intelligent and social animal
  • Lives in groups called troops
  • Communicates using sounds and gestures
  • ⚠️ Safety: Do not disturb or make loud noises
  • 🍌 Food: Eats fruits, leaves, and vegetables. Do not feed directly

Parrot

  • Colorful bird that can mimic sounds
  • Very intelligent and social
  • ⚠️ Safety: Do not touch wings or feathers
  • 🌾 Food: Eats seeds and fruits. Feed only under supervision

Giraffe

  • Tallest animal in the world
  • Has a very long neck
  • Feeds on leaves from tall trees
  • ⚠️ Safety: Stay calm and avoid sudden movements
  • 🥕 Food: Eats leaves and carrots. Feeding allowed in supervised areas

Rabbit

  • Small, cute, and calm animal
  • Very friendly, perfect for petting
  • ⚠️ Safety: Handle gently
  • 🥕 Food: Eats carrots and vegetables. Only supervised feeding allowed
